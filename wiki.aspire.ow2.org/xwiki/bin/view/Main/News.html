Recent Demonstrations of AspireRfid middleware and applications in Public events:

  • October 2009: Demonstration of Warehouse Management Processes (Receiving) over an RFID Dock-Door in the scope of the Athens Digital Week Festival-, Athens, Greece, October 16-18, 2009.
  • June 22, 2009: Aspire presented at OSGi DevCon 2009, Zurich. Presentation ...
  • June 3 and 4, 2009: demonstration of the OW2 Aspire project on the OW2 stand at JavaOne 2009. Diaporama ...
  • April 23, 2009: a team of UJF students is selected in the ten finalists (research track) of the NFC Forum Competition. Their project untitled "EasyRide - An Ecological, Ethical and Social Ride-Sharing NFC-Enabled System" is based on the NFC Components of the OW2 Aspire RFID project. The project was presented and demonstrated at the WIMA NFC Developers Summit (Monaco, April 23 2009).
  • April 2009: OW2 Aspire RFID proposes "Summer of Code" for the OW2 Programming Context.
  • April 2009: OW2 Aspire RFID : a privacy-friendly RFID middleware @ OW2Con 2009, Paris, April 1-2, 2009
  • February 2009: a team of UJF students submits a academic solution to the NFC Forum Competition based on the OW2 Aspire RFID components. "March 23, 2009: 20 finalists announced" and "April 23, 2009: Finalists present entries at WIMA NFC Developers Summit"
  • January 2009: AspireRfid Demonstration in the scope of ASPIRE Project Review Meeting Brussels, Belgium, January 20-21, 2009.
  • December 2008: (RFID Suite) Demonstration in the scope of the ACM Middleware 2008 conference, Leuwen, Belgium, December 1-5, 2008.
  • October 2008: (AIT Branch) Demonstration in the scope of the Athens Digital Week Festival-, Athens, Greece, October 15-19, 2008.
  • May 2008: (AIT Branch) Demonstration in the scope of the ASPIRE- RFID Information Day in Greece, Athens, Greece, May 21, 2008.
